Start Here

Start at Piece of Yoga to find what you need to take into your life.

Whether it is finding calm, relaxation, focus, or clarity, you will be able to find it here and perhaps even build on it throughout your day. Yoga to find calm. For one-on-one classes, there will be a free consultation offered to find out what your needs are. The yoga instructor will build the practice for what you are looking for. It is up to you to do a half hour session or one hour session. The group classes included about 10 people and usually follow a theme for the day such as kindness, happiness, and gratitude. We encourage everyone to try a group class. The experience of a group class includes getting to know your classmates, an introduction to the theme of the day from the yoga instructor, and reflection at the end of the yoga class.
